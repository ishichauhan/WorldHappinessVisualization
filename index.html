HAPPINESSAROUND WORLD

HAPPINESS IS THE HIGHEST FORM OF WELL BEING

CSC-196v - Final Project

By: Himanshu Chauhan and Reka

The World Happiness Report is a landmark survey of the state of global happiness. We present a set of interactive visualizations of the Happiness factor and other attributes determining the wellness of the countries in the world. The dataset shows the World Happiness factor for over a decade (2006-2016).

Happiness Factor

Happiness Factor is self-reported as the answer to the following question: “Please imagine a ladder, with steps numbered from 0 at the bottom to 10 at the top. The top of the ladder represents the best possible life for you and the bottom of the ladder represents the worst possible life for you. On which step of the ladder would you say you personally feel you stand at this time?”.

Other Factors

We took into account GDP per capita, Healthy Life Expectancy, Social Support, Freedom to make Life Choices, Confidence in National Government

How Happy Is Our World ?

The Happiness Score can be seen for a country on the globe by hovering to its location on the Globe. Compare Factors for 6 Countries

We compared the data of 6 countries out of the dataset of 150 countries, using line charts in d3. The way we came up with these 6 countries was that we choose the country with the highest, lowest, medium happiness score. For instance, Norway has the highest Happiness Score while Central-African-Republic has the lowest in the world.
